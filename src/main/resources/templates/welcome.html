Welcome to Hermes

A consumer should only need to scan a QR code in order to make a purchase.

  • Retailers pair Products and Prices in Hermes to form Offers.
  • Consumers that are interested in an Offer can scan it, placing an Order
  • Consumers confirm they will pay the Price, the Order becomes a Purchase.
  • A Purchase is finalized once Delivery has been made.
